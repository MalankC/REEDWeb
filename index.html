REED - Redefining the Way you Learn

Conventional methods of learning have always been a hassle.
Millions of children keep getting subjected to the same studying envirnonments, and the need of any singular kid is side-lined.
Thus, to remedy this, we bring modern technology into the mix.
Over the years, and especially since the 1980s, there is prominence and speedy development in the world of AI.

How we achieve it
